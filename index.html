Technology

State-of-the-Art Architectures

Our system is built on research-validated deep learning models and optimized for deployment on edge devices.

🧠

ResNet-50

Skip connections enable deep gradient flow. Benchmark: 98%+ on PlantVillage dataset.

🔬

DenseNet-121

Dense feature reuse with 8M params. Achieved 99.81% on fine-grained disease variants.

⚡

EfficientNet-B3

Compound scaling for optimal size-accuracy trade-off. Excels on PlantDoc field data.

👁️

Vision Transformer

Self-attention over image patches for global context. ViT-30 at 98.41% on Rice Leaf data.

📱

MobileNetV3

Quantized to 0.93M params with ~99.5% accuracy. Runs on-device with TFLite.

🔒

Federated Learning

Privacy-preserving distributed training. 93.5% accuracy without sharing raw farm data.

🎨

GANs / CycleGAN

Synthetic data generation to balance rare disease classes and bridge the lab-to-field gap.

💬

Multimodal LLMs

GPT-4o and CLIP for explainable diagnoses up to 98.12% accuracy with natural language.
